Experience the classic T-Rex runner game that's entertained millions

How to Play

Basic Controls

Press SPACE or UP ARROW to jump over obstacles. Use DOWN ARROW to duck under flying pterodactyls.

Scoring System

Your score increases the longer you survive. Every 100 points marks a new milestone, and the game speeds up!

Game Over

Hit any obstacle and it's game over. Press SPACE to restart and try to beat your high score!

Game Features

Progressive Difficulty

The game gradually increases in speed as you progress, testing your reflexes and timing.

Day/Night Cycle

Experience dynamic gameplay with changing environments as you reach higher scores.

Pro Tips

Timing is Everything

Learn to time your jumps perfectly. Jump early for tall cacti and late for short ones.

Watch the Pattern

Obstacles appear in patterns. Learning these patterns will help you achieve higher scores.
