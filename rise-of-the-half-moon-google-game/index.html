Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Half Moon Google Game

Is this the official Google Moon Doodle game?

Our game is inspired by Google's interactive moon landing doodle but expands it into a full strategy experience. You can play the original doodle via the link in our hero section.

Do I need a Google account to play?

While you can play without an account, signing in with Google allows progress saving, cloud backups, and access to leaderboards.

Is there multiplayer competition?

Yes! The persistent world includes thousands of players. You can form alliances, trade resources, or compete for territory.

How does this differ from the Google Doodle?

While the doodle focuses on the landing sequence, our game adds colony building, resource management, technology research, and multiplayer interaction.
